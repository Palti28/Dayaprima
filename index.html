    About Us

    Established on February 2019, PT. Daya Prima Mas originila vision was to provide valves products in the local market. We have now grown our product range to include Pipes and fittings, Expansion Joints and all steel material supply for Industrial or Contracting Projects.
    PT. Daya Prima Mas has a commitment in selling and procuring a Good and competitive products to large companies and contractors. Based On Cikarang, our products are sold throughout Indonesia.
